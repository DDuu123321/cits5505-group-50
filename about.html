About Us

We are Master's students at the University of Western Australia (UWA). This website is part of our CITS5505 Agile Web Development group project. StudyTime Tracker is designed to help students monitor and analyze their study patterns. It plays an important role in promoting productivity, maintaining balanced study routines, and improving academic success through structured analytics and sharing features. This application allows users to record their study activities and time, analyze study efficiency and patterns, and selectively share data analysis results with classmates, instructors, or study groups, helping to establish more effective study habits.

Key Features

Record Study Time

Track your study sessions with our timer or log them manually. Add details like subject and efficiency rating.

Analyze Performance

Visualize your study patterns, identify your most productive hours, and track progress toward your goals.

Share Progress

Create study groups, share insights with classmates, or keep your instructors updated on your progress.
